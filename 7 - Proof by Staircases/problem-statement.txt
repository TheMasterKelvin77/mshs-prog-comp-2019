Every day after math class, you climb the same staircase. Since you have long legs, you can take the steps one or two at a time. Naturally, your math class puts you in a mathy mood, and you wonder just how many different ways there are to climb the stairs, given the total number of steps.

Input Format

A single integer 0<N<10000 denoting the total number of steps on the staircase.

Output Format

A single integer denoting the number of possible ways to climb the stairs taking either one or two steps at a time.

Sample Input 0

1

Sample Output 0

1

Sample Input 1

2

Sample Output 1

2

Explanation 1

You can take the 2 steps at once, or you can take 1 step and then 1 step. That's 2 possibilities, so the output is 2.

Sample Input 2

5

Sample Output 2

8

Explanation 2

The 8 possibilities are:

1, 1, 1, 1, 1

1, 1, 1, 2

1, 1, 2, 1

1, 2, 1, 1

2, 1, 1, 1

1, 2, 2

2, 1, 2

2, 2, 1
